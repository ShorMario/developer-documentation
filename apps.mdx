## Why should I make an app

### If you are a company:

Building apps allows you to create hyper-niche experiences specific to your business. This enables great UX for your users, and differentiates you from competitors

### If you are a developer:

As you can monetize your apps, you can take a cut every time a company users your app. This is a great way to make money, without needing to run a business.
