## Why should I make an app

Creating apps offers impactful benefits whether you're a company looking for business-specific solutions or a developer eager to monetize your skills.

### Advantages for Companies

Developing your own apps allows your business to offer **hyper-niche** experiences catered to your unique needs. This approach not only enhances user experience (UX) but also sets you apart from your competition.

### Advantages for Developers

As you can monetize your apps, you can take a cut every time a company users your app. This is a great way to make money, without needing to run a business.
