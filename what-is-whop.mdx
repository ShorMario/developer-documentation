---
title: What is Whop
---

**Whop** is a marketplace for digital products. Whether you’re looking to sell a web app, software project, file, course, or access to a private community — Whop has the services to help you distribute your product.

Selling your product on Whop is a streamlined and simplistic process across all of our different use-cases - even offering the ability to bundle your offerings so that your customers get access to all of your offerings all at once. To get started creating your company and first product, click [here](https://docs.whop.com/creating-company).
